About IT-SAMAJ

IT-SAMAJ was created to help Nepali students discover IT courses, compare degree paths, and make confident career decisions.

Our Purpose

IT-SAMAJ is designed to make IT education easy to understand for students across Nepal. It offers clear comparisons, course summaries, and practical guidance on which IT degree may fit your goals.

The website helps translate academic choices into career outcomes, with a balanced view of study pressure, admission requirements, and job opportunities.

What You Will Find

On this site you can explore the main IT degrees in Nepal, compare BSc CSIT, BCA, BIT, BIM, and Computer Engineering, and access curated resources for each program.

The goal is to provide a consistent and easy-to-read guide that helps learners pick the right IT path.

Founder
